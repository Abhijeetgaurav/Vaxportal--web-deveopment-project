Get your booster dose

The booster dose is designed to reinforce your immune response and provide an extra layer of defense against emerging variants and potential waning immunity over time. It helps to further strengthen your body's ability to fight off the virus, reducing the risk of severe illness, hospitalization, and transmission.

VaxPortal is a government initiative dedicated to providing free COVID-19 vaccinations to the people of our nation. With a strong commitment to public health and the well-being of our citizens, VaxPortal aims to ensure that every individual has access to safe and effective vaccines. Our mission is to facilitate a seamless vaccination process, making it convenient for people to get immunized against the COVID-19 virus. Through our extensive network of vaccination centers and dedicated healthcare professionals, we strive to administer vaccines efficiently and prioritize the health and safety of the population.
